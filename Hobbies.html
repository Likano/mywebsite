Hobbies

Football


#KeepPushing.


2019 I lifted my first gold trophy after winning T'sakholo Moshoeshoe Games. In 2021 I joined Limkokwing football club and early 2022 in March I lifted my 2nd gold trophy after winning LUCSA(Lesotho Universities & Colleges Sports Association) Cup against National University of Lesotho(NUL) under the supervision of player coach Nketsi Mahanetsa.In April I lifted my 3rd silver trophy winning from penalty shoot out against Lerotholi Polytechnic(LP) on Traffic Lights Cup. In October independence day I lifted the 4th trophy after suffuring a final defeat against NUL on a Universities and colleges IEC Tournament that was held at Lesotho high school.
